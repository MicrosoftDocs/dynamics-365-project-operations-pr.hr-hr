<span data-ttu-id="a1f7e-106">Uključene komponente podliježu načinu naplate, podjelama kupca, ograničenjima koja se ne smiju prekoračiti i postavkama učestalosti računa definiranim u retku ponude koji se temelji na projektu.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a1f7e-106">Included components are subject to the billing method, customer splits, not-to-exceed limits, and invoice frequency settings defined on the project-based quote line.</span></span>

   - <span data-ttu-id="a1f7e-110">Nenaplativo</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a1f7e-110">Non-chargeable</span></span>
